üçì Summer Berry Chia Smoothie Bowl

Cool down with this refreshing and antioxidant-rich smoothie bowl!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ¬Ω cup almond milk (or plant-based milk of choice)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup

Toppings:

  • Fresh berries
  • Granola
  • Chia seeds
  • Coconut flakes
  • Sliced banana

Instructions:

  • In a blender, combine frozen berries, banana, almond milk, chia seeds, and maple syrup.
  • Blend until smooth and thick (use less milk for a thicker consistency).
  • Pour into a bowl and add your favorite toppings.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your colorful, nutrient-rich bowl!

Cozy up with a hearty bowl of lentil stew packed with seasonal veggies like sweet potatoes, carrots, and kale.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  • In a large pot, heat olive oil and saut√© onions and garlic until soft.
  • Add carrots, sweet potato, cumin, and paprika. Stir well.
  • Pour in the vegetable broth and lentils. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer for 25‚Äì30 minutes, or until lentils and veggies are tender.
  • Stir in chopped kale and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Add salt, pepper, and a splash of lemon juice to enhance the flavor.
  • Serve warm with whole grain bread or on its own.
